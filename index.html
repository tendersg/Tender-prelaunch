How Tender Works

Simple steps to get the best offers for what you need

1

Request

Consumer posts specific product request with requirements ("iPhone 15 Pro 256GB, new/sealed, delivery by Friday, budget SGD $1,400")

Buyer creates request
2

Notification

Verified suppliers in relevant category receive instant notification via dashboard and mobile app

Sellers compete for your business
3

Competitive Bidding

Real-time auction period (30-60 minutes) where suppliers submit initial bids and can lower bids to stay competitive. Suppliers see they're competing (competitive pressure indicator) but not exact competitor bids

Buyer selects best offer
4

Transparency

Consumer sees all bids in real-time with supplier ratings, delivery terms, warranty details. No hidden information. No surprise fees.

Consumer sees bids
5

Receive Bids

Consumer accepts best overall offer (not just lowest price - factors include supplier rating, delivery speed, warranty terms)

Consumer accepts offer
6

Secure Transaction

Payment held in escrow, released upon delivery confirmation. Built-in dispute resolution and buyer protection

Payment released upon delivery confirmation
